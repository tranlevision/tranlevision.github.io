Biography


I am a Ph.D. Student at Dept. of Electronics Engineering, Myongji University, and an AI Engineer at MindinTech, Inc. (Telechips), South Korea. My research concentrates on Computer Vision and Machine Learning.

I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering with an Excellent Degree Classification in 2018, majoring in Automation and Control Engineering, at HCMUTE, Vietnam. I spent one year being an intern and an AI Engineer at FPT Software, before being on the flight to South Korea in 2019. Earlier in 2017, I was a Teaching Assistant for one year and joined Intelligent Systems Lab, where I began my research journey and published my first research paper at the end of 2018. By far, I have been working with Prof. Dong-Chul Park, who has over 30-year experience in Machine Learning and has been a member of the Editorial Board for IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks.

I love beauty, sports, photography, and can not fail to mention art (music and drawing) which is always along with me as a great passion.

Last but not least, I always take care of my research due to my massive love for this work.
